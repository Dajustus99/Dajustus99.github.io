Online Gambling in Modern Times

Online gambling has increasingly become more relevant through online sports betting, casino apps and its inclusion in video games by way of loot boxes and gacha mechanics. This is not to mention "prediction markets" such as Kalshi or trends in retail gamification which are steadily saturating many aspects of life with gambling elements. Gambling is becoming normalized to a much greater degree than ever before.

Rise of Online Sports betting

The ruling of Murphy v. NCAA in 2018 took away the ability of the federal government to prevent states from legealizing sports gambling. Currently, as of April 2026, online sports betting is available in 30 states, with sports betting of any kind legal in 39.
