Gambling as a Behavioural Addiction

The understanding and research on gambling as an addiction is underserved when compared to substance-related addiction, and this is only beginning to change in recent years. In 2013 gambling was finally classified as behavorial addiciton, with that report noting its similarities to the effects of substance-related addictions.1
