The State of Modern Gambling Research and treatment

Unfortunately as it stands with the recent surge of online gambling and its relative recency in being treated as a behavioral addiction, there is little conclusive information on the effects of online gamebling, or the best treatment. One possible method is Congitive Behavior Therapy, which seeks to correct thought patterns and is thought to be one solution to gambling addiction. The evidence however, does not seem to support this method with any sort of authority.1

There are other more promising potential treatments such as Cognitive Control Training, but a lack of overall research and studies on a large scale, especially taking into account online gambling and how the constant access to online gambling might affect our responses to it.

This is also the case for both prevention and positive intereference from those that offer or advertise gambling services. There is a certain responsibility for these groups to try to mitigate problem gambling and keep their users knowledgable about the possible dangers. As online gambling continues to evolve and spread, who needs to be responsible and provide this aid should also evolve and include a wider range of groups as they propagate gambling, as should the level of aid they offer, to match the rising danger of online gambling.

If you feel you suffer from gambling addiction, know someone who does, or simply want to know more about the topic, please seek out additional information.
